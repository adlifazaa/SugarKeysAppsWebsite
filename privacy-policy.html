Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Sugar Keys Apps ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile applications and visit our website at sugarkeysapps.com. By using our apps or website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use our mobile applications:

  • Device information — including device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information to ensure compatibility and improve app performance.
  • Usage analytics — including how you interact with our apps, features used, session duration, and general usage patterns to help us understand and improve user experience.
  • Crash reports — including diagnostic data, error logs, and stack traces to help us identify and fix technical issues and improve app stability.
  • Advertising identifiers — including Google Advertising ID (GAID) on Android and similar identifiers used to deliver relevant advertisements and measure ad performance.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our mobile applications
  • Monitor and analyze usage trends and app performance
  • Diagnose and fix technical problems and crashes
  • Deliver and measure the effectiveness of advertisements
  • Respond to user support requests and inquiries
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

Services Used

Our applications may use the following third-party services to provide functionality and analytics:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our apps. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. For more information, visit Google's Privacy Policy.

Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our apps. This service collects usage data and device information. Learn more at Firebase Privacy Information.

Firebase Crashlytics

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and diagnostic data to improve app stability. Crashlytics may collect device state information at the time of a crash. Learn more at Firebase Privacy Information.

Advertising

Our mobile applications may display advertisements served by Google AdMob. These ads may be personalized based on your interests and advertising identifiers, or they may be non-personalized depending on your settings and applicable regulations.

Third-party advertisers may use cookies, advertising identifiers, and similar technologies to collect information about your device and app usage to deliver relevant ads and measure ad effectiveness. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Retention

We retain collected information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Analytics and crash data are typically retained according to the retention policies of the third-party services we use.

Children's Privacy

Our applications are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we collect. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Links

Our apps or website may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access the personal information we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate information
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information
  • The right to opt out of personalized advertising
  • The right to object to or restrict certain processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights or to make a privacy-related inquiry, please contact us using the information below. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
